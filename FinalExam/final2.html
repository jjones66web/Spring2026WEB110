Japanese Pop Music

This page is about Japanese Pop music of the 1980s. City Pop is Japanese music between middle of 1970s to the 1980s that are music sytles of funk, synth, disco/boogie, with some soft rock. I ended up getting into this genre of music due to it being kind of similar to the music I grew up listening to here in America. It was nice to hear something fresh instead of the same repeated songs on the radio and everyday play. I think that is what ultimately catched my interest
