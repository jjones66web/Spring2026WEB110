PC ENGINE!

This page is about the PC ENGINE. The PC Engine was originally released in 1987 in Japan as a video game console and used a credit card style for it's form of media instead of a standard cartridge like the Nintendo Entertain System and Sega Master System. The system was released later in the US markets as the Turbo Grafx 16.

SPECS!

Hardware Specifications and Games

The case for the PC Engine is a very small console for it's time that is a small white box, while other releases come in a grey shell. The middle area has a card slot with the front having a power and one controller connector. On the side is the RF(video and sound) module and the back area has an expansion port.


Games

A small list of games that were released for the console.

  • ROCK ON!
  • Kung Fu
  • Pac Land
  • Keith Courage In Alpha Zone
  • GunHed
  • Space Harrier
